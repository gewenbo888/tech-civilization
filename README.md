# Tech Civilization Atlas · 科技文明图谱

A civilisation-scale, fully bilingual (EN | 中文) atlas of the world's technology centers —
how innovation hubs emerge, concentrate intelligence, compete, and reshape human history.

> Innovation does not spread evenly — it pools. Technology centers are modern civilisation's
> brains: intelligence concentrated into geographic form.
>
> 创新并非均匀铺开——它会汇聚。科技中心，是现代文明的大脑——被压缩成地理形态的智能。

A global language toggle switches every label, map, diagram, ranking and essay between English
and Chinese without a reload.

## The ten systems

1. **Global Tech-Hub Map** — interactive planetary map, nodes sized by composite power + flow arcs
2. **China's Multi-Core System** — Beijing/Shenzhen/Shanghai/Hangzhou/Hefei division of labour
3. **The Silicon Valley Civilisation** — Stanford, VC, failure culture, the exported playbook
4. **Semiconductor Geopolitics** — an interactive supply chain (materials → ASML → TSMC → GPU clusters)
5. **University & Talent Networks** — an elite-university graph of "talent reactors"
6. **Internet & Platform Civilisation** — when companies became infrastructure
7. **Military & Technology** — DARPA, dual-use, the forcing function of rivalry
8. **City Operating Systems** — a 9-axis radar comparing any two hubs head-to-head
9. **Future Tech Centers** — compute gravity, energy as the binding limit, new entrants
10. **The Civilisation Technology Index** — a sortable Bloomberg-style leaderboard

Plus a 16-hub gallery: Silicon Valley, Beijing, Shenzhen, Shanghai, Hangzhou, Hefei, Boston,
Tokyo, Seoul, Taipei, Bangalore, London, Berlin, Singapore, Tel Aviv, Zurich — each scored
across nine power dimensions (talent, capital, scientific infrastructure, manufacturing, energy,
compute, education, geopolitical stability, information flow).

## Note

The Civilisation Technology Index is an editorial composite of public-domain knowledge —
illustrative estimates for comparison, not live measurements or investment guidance.
